About Me#

I’m Simone Sturniolo. I’m a software developer working in London. I got a PhD in physics back in Italy, at the University of Pavia, and then worked for a long time as computational scientist, writing code to help solve problems of atomic structure in solid matter. Now I’m working at a start-up developing a new biomedical device.

In my personal projects I usually experiment for fun with building interesting things or exploring scientific ideas in domains like dynamical systems. I also occasionally develop games and may do some creative writing.
